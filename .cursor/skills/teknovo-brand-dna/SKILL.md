# Teknovo Brand DNA

**Scope**: Canonical brand identity for all Teknovo surfaces — ERP portals, public landing pages, and marketing. Load **before** creative direction, product design, or UI implementation.

**Downstream**: **teknovo-creative-director** (visual review) · **teknovo-product-designer** (experience design) · **teknovo-design-system** (tokens)

---

## Responsibilities

Menjadi sumber identitas visual Teknovo.

Menentukan:

- Brand personality
- Visual language
- Voice and tone
- Storytelling principles
- Photography direction
- Color philosophy
- Component personality

---

## Brand Personality

Teknovo adalah:

- Modern Vocational School
- Industry-Oriented
- Technology-Driven
- Future Workforce Builder
- Professional Yet Human

---

## Forbidden Brand Direction

Jangan pernah menghasilkan:

- Generic school template
- Wordpress style
- Government website style
- Crypto/Web3 style
- Startup hype style
- AI-generated marketing style

---

## Visual Language

| Dimension | Teknovo Expression |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Layout | Editorial — headline → supporting copy → primary action → secondary detail |
| Density | Scan-in-5-seconds; premium whitespace over card spam |
| Color | Semantic, restrained — blue for trust/action; no decorative gradients |
| Typography | Geist headings (landing) · Inter body — confident, readable |
| Imagery | Real students, workshops, industry partners — no AI-generated faces |
| Components | Purposeful, calm — not decorative widgets |

---

## Voice and Tone

- **Professional Yet Human** — speak to parents, students, and industry partners directly
- **Indonesian-first** — clear labels; reject jargon and AI fluff
- **Evidence-based** — real stats, real outcomes, real school identity
- **Never generic** — no "Selamat datang" without answering *why choose this school*

---

## Storytelling Principles

1. Lead with **outcome** (career readiness, industry skills) — not features
2. Anchor every page in **school identity** — programs, partners, achievements
3. One primary narrative thread per page — no competing stories
4. Human rhythm — alternate dense information with breathing room

---

## Photography Direction

- Authentic vocational contexts: labs, workshops, internships, competitions
- Diverse, real students and teachers — never stock-photo generic
- Industry partnership visuals when available
- Reject: AI faces, over-saturated filters, corporate stock clichés

---

## Color Philosophy

- Blue = trust, education, action — not decoration
- Neutrals carry content — white/slate rhythm on landing pages
- Status colors semantic only (success, warning, danger)
- Reject: gradient abuse, glassmorphism, neon accents, purple SaaS palettes

---

## Component Personality

| Context | Personality |
|---------|-------------|
| ERP portal | Operator-first — tables, lists, calm density |
| Landing page | Editorial storytelling — not dashboard marketing |
| Forms | Clear labels, single primary action |
| Cards | Earned placement — not KPI spam |
