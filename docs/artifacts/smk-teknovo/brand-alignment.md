**Date:** 2026-06-21

**PRD Baseline:** V2.1

## Positioning Reset

SMK Teknovo is no longer framed as a generic vocational school portal.  
For PRD V2.1, the platform must consistently present SMK Teknovo as a **Digital Experience Platform** and a **Future Workforce Academy**.

**Primary message (V2.1):** **Belajar. Berkarya. Siap Industri.**

**Design order:** Story First → Motion Second → 3D Third → Technology Last

## Brand Personality (PRD V2.1)

| Trait | PRD V2.1 Expression |
|-------|---------------------|
| Future Workforce Academy | Students are introduced as future professionals, not passive school visitors |
| Industry-Oriented | Every chapter must connect learning to real workforce demand and employability |
| Professional Yet Human | Confident and credible, but still warm to students and parents |
| Spatially Modern | The experience feels advanced, cinematic, and designed with intent |
| Outcome-Led | The story must lead toward transformation, readiness, and PPDB conversion |

## Strategic Shift from Legacy Narrative

| Area | Legacy / Interim | PRD V2.1 Direction |
|------|------------------|---------------------|
| Positioning | Vocational portal / school story | Digital Experience Platform / Future Workforce Academy |
| Main programs | `TKJ / RPL / DKV` | `Teknik Mesin + Usaha Layanan Wisata` |
| Core promise | School facilities and majors | Birthplace of future professionals |
| Story framing | Chapter-based school promo | Workforce narrative with cinematic progression |
| Primary message | Generic school excellence | **Belajar. Berkarya. Siap Industri.** |

## Visual Language

- **Canvas:** deep, premium, dark-forward surfaces that feel engineered rather than brochure-like.
- **Depth:** motion, light, and layered perspective should imply progression into the future of work.
- **Typography:** editorial hierarchy first; one dominant message per viewport.
- **Imagery:** real vocational, workshop, hospitality, and industry contexts; never generic stock-school imagery.
- **Composition:** disciplined focal-point layouts inspired by Apple, Framer, Linear, Stripe, and Awwwards principles, without copying any specific layout.

## Voice and Tone

### Must sound like

- "Tempat masa depan profesional dimulai."
- "Belajar tidak berhenti di kelas, tetapi berlanjut ke kesiapan kerja."
- "Teknik Mesin dan ULW ditampilkan sebagai jalur menuju dunia industri dan layanan nyata."
- **"Belajar. Berkarya. Siap Industri."** — used as the governing headline system.

### Must not sound like

- "Selamat datang di website resmi sekolah kami."
- "Sekolah unggulan dengan berbagai fasilitas lengkap."
- Broad motivational copy that does not connect to workforce outcomes.

## Chapter-Level Brand Fit (V2.1)

The eight PRD V2.1 chapters must reinforce the same identity:

1. **Future Starts Here** — opening declaration of future workforce ambition.
2. **Industry Challenge** — market urgency and labor relevance.
3. **Teknik Mesin** — craft, fabrication, systems thinking, industrial readiness.
4. **Usaha Layanan Wisata** — service excellence, hospitality, professionalism, experience design.
5. **Industry Alignment** — school, tools, partners, and pathways are interconnected.
6. **Student Transformation** — growth from learner to job-ready individual.
7. **Achievements** — proof that the promise is earned.
8. **PPDB** — conversion path into the academy.
