# Creative Direction Review — SMK Teknovo Immersive Experience Platform

**Date:** 2026-06-21  
**Gate:** Creative Director  
**PRD Baseline:** V2.1  
**Verdict:** APPROVE

## Creative Thesis

The public experience should feel like stepping into a **future workforce academy**, not browsing a school homepage. The platform must blend cinematic scroll storytelling, editorial hierarchy, and purposeful spatial scenes so visitors quickly understand that Teknovo prepares students for real professional futures.

**Primary message:** **Belajar. Berkarya. Siap Industri.**

## Design Order (V2.1)

Story First → Motion Second → 3D Third → Technology Last

| Layer | Role | Gate |
|-------|------|------|
| Story | Narrative spine, copy hierarchy, chapter purpose | Must pass before motion work |
| Motion | Scroll choreography, transition language, pacing | Must pass before 3D scene build |
| 3D | Spatial proof for flagship program chapters | Must serve story, never lead it |
| Technology | Framework, performance, deploy | Last — never drives creative decisions |

## Core Emotional Direction

The experience should communicate:

- ambition,
- industrial relevance,
- modern professionalism,
- human transformation,
- and conversion readiness.

It should never feel like a school brochure, dashboard-themed landing page, or startup template in education clothing.

## Opening Scene Direction

**Narrative question to answer:**  
**"Why does the future start here?"**

### Approve

- An immersive opening composition with one dominant focal object or scene system.
- A visual statement that frames Teknovo as a launch point into real professions.
- Motion and 3D that reveal future-work themes before any deep reading is required.
- Primary message **Belajar. Berkarya. Siap Industri.** visible in the first viewport.

### Reject

- Flat hero banners.
- Logo-on-gradient openings.
- Headline + CTA + stock photo layouts.
- Generic "welcome" framing.

## Chapter Art Direction (PRD V2.1)

| Chapter | Art Direction | What it must feel like |
|---------|---------------|------------------------|
| 1. Future Starts Here | Spatial opening, premium dark canvas, strong focal object | A professional future is beginning now |
| 2. Industry Challenge | Editorial urgency with kinetic data rhythm | Industry demand is real and immediate |
| 3. Teknik Mesin | Mechanical precision, fabrication cues, industrial materiality | Hands-on engineering and production readiness |
| 4. Usaha Layanan Wisata | Service choreography, hospitality polish, experiential warmth | Professional service and human-facing excellence |
| 5. Industry Alignment | Connected nodes, partner pathways, systems map | Teknovo is connected to a wider workforce network |
| 6. Student Transformation | Before/after progression, layered reveal | Students become confident future professionals |
| 7. Achievements | Editorial proof, earned spotlight, selective prestige | The promise is backed by evidence |
| 8. PPDB | Focused action space, conversion clarity, confidence | Joining feels immediate, premium, and clear |

## Visual References (Principles Only)

Use these references for principles, not mimicry:

- **Apple:** ruthless hierarchy, one focal idea per frame.
- **Framer:** spatial scroll continuity and product-like pacing.
- **Linear:** precision, restraint, dark-surface discipline.
- **Stripe:** confident typography and controlled complexity.
- **Awwwards:** immersion, pacing, and distinctiveness without gimmicks.

## What to Avoid

- Feature-grid storytelling.
- KPI blocks as the primary proof language.
- Random futuristic particles or meaningless floating geometry.
- Generic education site tropes: smiling stock photo, badges, "why choose us" cards.
- Mixing `TKJ / RPL / DKV` with PRD V2.1 chapter focus in the same primary story.
- Building 3D scenes before story and motion gates pass.

Creative direction is approved for PRD V2.1. The experience should now be art-directed as a **workforce-launch narrative** with cinematic depth, chapter continuity, and clear distinction from the legacy `TKJ / RPL / DKV` implementation.
